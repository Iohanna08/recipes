ingredients for 1 person:
- 1 red beet
- olive oil (1 tbs)
- vinegar (2 tbs)
- honey (1 tbs)
- 1 apple
- 10 gr walnuts
- 20 gr goat cheese

Cook the beet in boiling water until it turns softer (approx. 30-40 minutes) and let it cool off
cut the apple in small pieces (julienne), you can peel it if you wish
make a sauce by mixing the honey, the vinegar and the oil
cut the beet the same way as the apple
mix the beet, apple and sauce together
on the top, sprinkle with some walnuts and pieces of goat cheese
